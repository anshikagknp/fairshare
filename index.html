❓ FAQ

good to know
Is the currency in Rupees?

Yes — every amount in FairShare is entered and displayed in ₹ (INR), formatted with standard Indian digit grouping.

Where is my trip data stored?

Signed-in accounts save to a secure database tied to your login. Guest mode saves only to this browser, on this device.

Can I edit an expense after adding it?

Not yet — delete the entry from Expense History and re-add it with the correct details. Balances update instantly either way.

How does "Settle Up" pick who pays whom?

A greedy algorithm matches the biggest debtor with the biggest creditor, repeating until everyone's even — the fewest possible payments.

What happens if I remove a crew member?

Their share is instantly recalculated across every logged expense, and any bill they paid for is removed to keep totals accurate.

Do unequal splits have to add up exactly?

Yes — FairShare blocks submission until every person's share sums to the exact bill total, down to the paisa.
